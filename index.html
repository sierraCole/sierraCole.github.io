Guyanese Pine Tarts

Pineapple Jam Filling

  • 1 can Crushed Pineapple
  • 1/2 cup Sugar + 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon, 1/4 tsp Nutmeg
  • 1 tsp Vanilla

Cook everything (except vanilla) for 45 mins until thick. Stir in vanilla. Cool completely.

Pastry Dough

  • 3 1/2 cups Flour
  • 1/3 cup Shortening + 1/3 cup Butter
  • 2 tsp Sugar + pinch of Salt
  • 3/4 cup Ice Water

Mix dry ingredients. Cut in butter + shortening. Add water, form dough, chill.

Assembly & Bake

  • Egg + 1 tbsp Water (egg wash)

Roll dough into circles, add pineapple jam, fold into triangles. Brush with egg wash. Bake at 350°F for 20–25 mins.

Enjoy Your Pine Tarts!
